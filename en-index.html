Welcome to Hiraku

Our textbook embraces a unique approach to learning, fostering collaboration between Japanese and English learners. Rooted in the philosophy of service learning, Hiraku offers an innovative way for learners to grow together.

Imagine you're studying Japanese at a university in the U.S. In class, you're mastering hiragana, katakana, and kanji, along with grammar and pronunciation. Beyond the classroom, you're immersing yourself in Japanese media, absorbing the language firsthand. But there's more to learn. You're curious about real-life usage: When and how do native speakers use phrases? Can you express yourself more naturally in Japanese? You crave authentic communication.

Likewise, Japanese students learning English seek the same authenticity. They long to understand how English is used in everyday life in the U.S. They're eager to converse fluently, just as you are in Japanese.

Hiraku empowers you to become both a teacher and a learner. Pair up with a study buddy and embark on a journey of mutual discovery. Through conversations, reflect on your own culture and society, while exploring your buddy's perspective. Utilize your classroom knowledge to benefit each other, fostering a community of shared learning and growth.

As you delve into Hiraku, you'll encounter stories to share, questions to ponder, and topics to discuss. Embrace the opportunity to craft your unique Hiraku experience with your study buddy.
