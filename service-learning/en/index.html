What is Service learning?

Service Learning is all about learning while making a difference in our communities. It's a hands-on approach where we share what we know while also learning from the people we're helping. In this language learning textbook, "Hiraku," we apply this idea to learning languages. You work with a Japanese native to improve your Japanese, while they enhance their English, creating a mutual learning experience.

In service learning, we follow a simple cycle: prepare, exchange, then reflect on what we've learned. In "Hiraku," you'll do the same. Before each session, get ready, then dive into the activities with your language partner. Afterward, take some time to think about what you've discovered and jot down your thoughts in the "Memo & Reflection" section. You can write about anything that stands out to you, whether it's what you've learned, questions you have, or experiences you've shared with your study buddy. Through this process, you'll not only improve your language skills but also grow personally and become more involved in your community.
